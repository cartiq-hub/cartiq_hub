How it Works

Your health score is an objective calculation based on three world-class peer-reviewed scientific standards.

Single Source of Truth: CartIQ Scoring

Score = Nutri-Score (60%) + NOVA Group (30%) + Additives (10%)
  • Nutri-Score (60%): Measures overall nutritional density based on sugar, salt, and protein.
  • NOVA Group (30%): Identifies processing levels from fresh to ultra-processed.
  • Additive Guard (10%): Flags 27+ restricted additives based on EFSA, IARC, & EPA standards.
All scores are calculated using lab-verified data from OpenFoodFacts combined with EFSA, IARC, and EPA regulatory standards. Scientific red flags include E171, nitrites, and aspartame.
